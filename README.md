# LeeWay Voice Scheduling Translator CRM

A mobile-first field outreach CRM for LeeWay Industries / LeeWay Innovations.

## What it does

- Voice capture for walk-ins, calls, follow-ups, and research notes
- Lead tracking by category, contact, role, phone, email, stage, offer, and next action
- Pipeline tracking from Identified to Closed Won
- Regional scheduling and route discipline
- 6-mile daily working-radius planning
- Mileage and fuel-cost estimates
- Translator / conversation helper for field outreach
- Activity log for every contact
- CSV and JSON export for daily backups

## How to use on phone

Open the GitHub Pages site in Chrome or Edge on Android.

Then use:

- **Voice Capture** after every visit or call
- **Today’s Route** for fast walk-in capture
- **Regional Scheduler** to plan routes by territory
- **Translator** when someone prefers another language
- **Import / Export** at the end of every day to back up your data

## Important

Data is stored in the browser using local storage. Export JSON daily to protect your CRM records.
