Voice Field Capture

Press record, speak naturally, then let LeeWay parse the details into the CRM.

Voice works best in Chrome or Edge. If the browser blocks microphone access, type or paste your spoken note into the transcript box and press parse.

Speak Like This

“I just walked into Ballers Club Barbershop on 27th Street. I spoke with Marcus, the manager. Phone is 414-555-1234. Email is marcus@example.com. They care about no-shows, memberships, reviews, and social media. I pitched the $250 AI Tune-Up. He said follow up tomorrow at 10 AM. Stage should be meeting booked.”

It will extract

  • Organization and category
  • Contact name and role
  • Phone, email, website
  • Activity type: walk-in, call, inbound call, email, research
  • Offer pitched and potential value
  • Pipeline stage and next follow-up
  • Pain points and conversation notes
